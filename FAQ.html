Frequently Asked Questions


What are Coronaviruses?


Coronaviruses (CoV) are a large family of viruses that cause illness ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS-CoV) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS-CoV).


What are the species of coronaviruses that have been discovered so far and that humans can be infected with from an animal source?


The SARS-CoV was transmitted from civet cats to humans in 2002 in China and MERS-CoV from dromedary camels to humans in 2012 in Saudi Arabia. Also, the novel coronavirus (COVID-19) had some link to the seafood and animal market in Wuhan, China.



Abstract


Problem Definition: - Wasting money on items that you use few times in a long period of time (for example: drills, Ladders and Grills). Existing solution: - Some shops can rent items and you give it back after you use them. Disadvantages of this solution: - Wasting time to go get the item from the shop and asking for a lot of information and If you damaged the time you need to pay it price. Our solution: - A website that you can lend and borrow items for free or you can donate your items. Minimum features: - Borrower can search for the items they are looking for. - Display some information like: the name of owner/borrower, showing the location of the owner and the borrower. - Owners can add a picture of the item. - The owner can rate the experiment he had with the borrower and other owners can see the rating of that borrower to accept his request to borrow their items. Optional features: - Customize the items into 2 categories: Loaned items and Donated items. - Dark/white theme
